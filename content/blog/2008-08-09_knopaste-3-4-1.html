--- title: "KNopaste 3.4.1" date: "2008-08-09T18:28:00+00:00" slug: "knopaste-3-4-1" --- Just a few minutes ago I released a new version of my KNopaste. The new version got the version number 3.4.1 and is - as usual - available via download from the sourceforge servers.

The changes in this version are limited to a new version of the GeSHi highlight engine. They released version 1.0.8 yesterday with some bug fixes, a faster engine and nine new supported languages. So if you were missing your favorite language at my KNopaste you might be happy after looking at the new version.

Other archive formats than the linked zip file are available at the download page since this new version.

If you watch some bugs crawling through my application please use the bug-tracker at sourceforge to let me know about them. I'll hunt and kill them after receiving your report.
